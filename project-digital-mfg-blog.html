About the Blog

A technical blog documenting my MSc research in digital manufacturing, including QR-code based traceability systems, digital twins, and process capability analysis. All content is version-controlled on GitHub and written in HTML/CSS from scratch.

Statistical analysis of dimensional accuracy across 50+ printed parts. Achieved Cpk > 1.33 for key features after process optimisation (nozzle temp, print speed, cooling).

Using Tecnomatix Plant Simulation to model a 50,200 units/year assembly line. Optimised bottleneck stations and achieved 87% OEE.
