Client Requirements & Delivery

The client needed a professional online presence to attract new clients and showcase their services. Key requirements:

  • ✅ Professional, calming design (holistic healing brand identity)
  • ✅ Service listings (healing therapies, training, card readings)
  • ✅ Contact information + free consultation booking
  • ✅ Testimonials section (client feedback)
  • ✅ Mobile-responsive design
  • ✅ Easy-to-update content structure

Delivered pages: Home, About, Therapies, Training, Readings, Events, Testimonials, Contact, Terms & Conditions.

Technical Implementation

The site was built with vanilla HTML/CSS/JS – no frameworks – ensuring fast load times and complete control over the user experience.

Site Structure:
crystalserenity.org.uk/
├── index.html          # Homepage with hero and services

├── training.html       # Workshops and courses
├── readings.html       # Card reading services
├── events.html         # Calendar and events
├── testimonials.html   # Client feedback
├── contact.html        # Contact form + map
├── terms.html          # Legal pages
├── images/             # Optimised assets
└── css/                # Custom styles

Key Features

  • Responsive design: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
  • Contact system: Email links + phone click-to-call
  • Service highlighting: Clear calls-to-action for bookings
  • Client testimonials: Social proof section
  • Free consultation offer: Prominent CTA
